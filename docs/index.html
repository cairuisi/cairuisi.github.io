About Me

I’m a second year Ph.D. student in the VITA Group of Electrical and Computer Engineering Department, the Univeristiy of Texas at Austin, under the supervision of Prof. Zhangyang (Atlas) Wang. Prior to that, I obatined my B.E. degree from University of Science and Technology of China (USTC). [CV]

I’m currently working on machine learning , with research focus on:

  • Efficient Model Training: Mixture of Experts (MoE), Model Merging & Recycling
  • Trustworthy Machine Learning System: Adversarial Robustness, Data Poisoning, Federated Learning
